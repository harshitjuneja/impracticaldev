# Welcome To Impractical Dev

This blog is where I write short and practical notes on:

- Data science experiments that are worth repeating.
- Health AI and medical imaging workflows.
- Engineering trade-offs from production systems.

## How This Blog Works

Posts are written in Markdown and rendered on the website. To publish a new post:

1. Add a new `.md` file in `content/posts/`
2. Add one entry to `content/posts/index.json`
3. Reload the blog page

That is it.

## What To Expect

I care less about hype and more about repeatable outcomes. Most posts will include:

- Problem setup
- Constraints
- What failed
- What worked
- What I would change next

See you in the next post.
